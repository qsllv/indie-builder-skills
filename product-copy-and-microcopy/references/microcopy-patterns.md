# Microcopy Patterns

## Buttons

Use action + object or outcome.

- "Upload CSV"
- "Create report"
- "Send invite"
- "Analyze feedback"
- "Export results"

Avoid:

- Submit
- Continue when the destination is unclear
- Click here

## Empty States

Formula:
"No [object] yet. [Action] to [value]."

Examples:

- "No reports yet. Create your first report to track weekly growth."
- "No feedback imported yet. Upload a CSV to group messages by theme."

## Errors

Formula:
"We could not [action] because [reason]. [Recovery action]."

Examples:

- "We could not save changes because your session expired. Sign in again and retry."
- "The file is over 20MB. Upload a smaller CSV or remove unused columns."

## Success

Formula:
"[Object/action] completed. [Useful next step]."

Examples:

- "Import complete. 184 messages were added and 6 rows need review."
- "Invite sent. Alex will join this workspace after accepting the email."

## Confirmation

Formula:
"[Action] [object]? This will [impact]. [Undo status]."

Examples:

- "Delete 'June feedback import'? This removes 184 messages from this workspace. This cannot be undone."
- "Cancel subscription? Your Pro access remains active until July 1."

## Upgrade Prompts

Formula:
"You've reached [limit]. Upgrade to [plan] to [paid value]."

Examples:

- "You've analyzed 100 feedback messages this month. Upgrade to Pro to analyze up to 5,000 and export results."

Avoid shame, fake urgency, or hiding the free path.

## AI States

Examples:

- "Analyzing feedback. This usually takes under a minute."
- "The result may miss themes if the input is short or inconsistent."
- "Review the draft before sending it to customers."
