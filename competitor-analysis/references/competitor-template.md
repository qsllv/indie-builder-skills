## Review Mining

Look for:

- Setup complaints.
- Pricing complaints.
- Missing integrations.
- Too complex or too limited.
- Poor output quality.
- Slow support.
- Trust or privacy concerns.
- Feature bloat.

## Wedge Examples

AI feedback analyzer:

- Competitors may be broad product discovery platforms.
- Wedge: "Upload feedback and get ranked themes in 5 minutes, no full feedback portal setup."
- Avoid: building roadmap voting, team permissions, and enterprise integrations first.

Resume tool:

- Competitors may be full resume builders.
- Wedge: "Analyze one resume against one job description instantly."
- Avoid: template marketplace and full career platform.

Developer tool:

- Competitors may be enterprise platforms.
- Wedge: "Works locally with one command for solo developers."
- Avoid: enterprise admin dashboard first.
